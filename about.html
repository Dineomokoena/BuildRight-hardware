Our Story

Building trust since 1985

From Humble Beginnings

BuildRight Hardware was founded in 1985 by John and Mary Builder with a single storefront in downtown Construction City. What began as a small family business has grown into the region's premier supplier of construction materials.

Our commitment to quality products, fair pricing, and exceptional customer service has remained unchanged through three decades of growth.
